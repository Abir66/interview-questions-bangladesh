Recently I'm interviewed at WellDev for **Trainee Software Engineer Position (ColdFusion)**. WellDev Ltd is a software company based in Zurich, Switzerland, specializing in software development and IT services. It has offices in Bangladesh (Dhaka), South Africa, Canada etc.

## Interview Stages

1. **Initial Screening:** I applied through online after seeing their post in linkedin. They took a MCQ Round, almost every candidate got an email for participation in this round.
2. **First Technical Round:** An HR handed over the interview to a Junior Software Engineer and interview lasted for about an hour.
3. **Second Technical Round and HR Interview:** Two software engineer conducted the technical sessions. But before the technical round, there was a 20 minutes HR round asking questions like why you chose WellDev, what is your future plan, what are your biggest strength etc.
4. **COO Round:** I was rejected before this round. Hopefully some other will contribute on it later.

## Interview Questions in Each Subsequent Round

### MCQ Round: A Broad Range of Topics

This round consisted of a multiple-choice questionnaire covering these topics. The test required me to share my screen with Quillgo and keep my camera on. It covers wide range of topics like JavaScript fundamentals, OOP, DBMS, SWE principles, Networking, Rest API knowledge, Analytical reasoning, DSA (time complexity, sorting, binary trees, MST, greedy algorithms).
